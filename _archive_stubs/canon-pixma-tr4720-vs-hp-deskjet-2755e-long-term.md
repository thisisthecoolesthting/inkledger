After 18 months of tracking two of Amazon's best-selling budget inkjet printers, the data reveals some uncomfortable truths about long-term costs. Here's what our test households discovered:

## The Contenders
- **[Canon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B079Y6FHKJ?tag=inkledger-20) PIXMA TR4720**: $99 starter price, uses PG-245/CL-246 cartridges
- **HP [DeskJet 2755e](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08H4YTTLP?tag=inkledger-20)**: $129 starter price, uses HP 67/67XL cartridges

## Key Findings
1. **First-Year [Cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GLTX2PNR?tag=inkledger-20) Replacements**
   - [Canon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00TRDOYLW?tag=inkledger-20): 3.2 color + 1.8 [black](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FKBFH92Q?tag=inkledger-20) cartridges (avg.)
   - HP: 2.9 color + 2.1 [black](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B081GRVJ5K?tag=inkledger-20) cartridges (avg.)

2. **OEM Ink Costs**
   | [Printer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B077QVSKD5?tag=inkledger-20) | [Black](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07NPHDS24?tag=inkledger-20) (per ml) | Color (per ml) | Annual Cost |
   |---------|---------------|---------------|-------------|
   | [Canon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08J3NY6QV?tag=inkledger-20)   | $0.99         | $1.12         | $187        |
   | HP      | $1.47         | $1.63         | $243        |

3. **[Compatible](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQ4VHBMH?tag=inkledger-20) Alternatives**
   - [InkWorks PG-245/CL-246 4-pack](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0G1R37VJC?tag=inkledger-20) cuts [Canon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07TJ87YKB?tag=inkledger-20) costs by 62%
   - [Jettec HP 67XL high-yield](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FVY8TTVN?tag=inkledger-20) reduces HP expenses by 58%

## The Hidden Costs
- **HP Instant Ink Trap**: The [2755e](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CT2R7199?tag=inkledger-20) aggressively pushes subscriptions (starting at $2.99/month) but limits pages
- **[Canon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DKJDRLTZ?tag=inkledger-20)'s Chip Games**: TR4720 cartridges sometimes report 'empty' prematurely, though reset tricks exist

## Verdict
While the HP prints marginally sharper photos, the Canon wins on:
- Lower baseline ink costs
- Wider third-party [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01BYKUI9C?tag=inkledger-20) support
- No forced subscription model

For light users (<100 pages/month), the TR4720 with [refill kits](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DVT2JTRN?tag=inkledger-20) delivers the most pain-free experience. Heavy users should consider laser alternatives.
