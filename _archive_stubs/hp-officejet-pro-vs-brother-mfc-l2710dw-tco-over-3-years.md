## The Inkjet vs Laser Showdown

When choosing between HP's [OfficeJet](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CFM82NS2?tag=inkledger-20) Pro inkjet printers and [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B06XY6RSNB?tag=inkledger-20)'s MFC-L2710DW laser [printer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B077QVSKD5?tag=inkledger-20), the upfront price is just the beginning. Here's how the costs stack up over 3 years for a typical home office printing 500 pages/month:

### Hardware Costs
- **[HP OfficeJet Pro](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08NQSHX3N?tag=inkledger-20) [9015e](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B092T3GBWK?tag=inkledger-20) (B07VVK39F7)**: $299
- **[Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01N4K0ZFE?tag=inkledger-20) MFC-L2710**: $349

### Consumables (3-Year Projection)
| Component       | HP OfficeJet Pro | [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DS4M632H?tag=inkledger-20) MFC-L2710DW |
|----------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Ink/[Toner](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B075X6C5ZW?tag=inkledger-20)      | $1,287           | $492                |
| Paper (5,000 sheets) | $150         | $150                |
| Maintenance Kits | $0             | $89                 |
| **Total**      | **$1,736**       | **$731**            |

### Key Findings
1. **[Toner](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07YGL9XTH?tag=inkledger-20) Wins on Volume**: [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GFMH3D3X?tag=inkledger-20)'s TN-760 high-yield [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GLTX2PNR?tag=inkledger-20) (B08616PS69) prints ~3,000 pages for $92 vs HP's 902XL [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01BYKUI9C?tag=inkledger-20) at $45 for just 825 pages.
2. **Inkjet Hidden Costs**: HP's automatic [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FKBFH92Q?tag=inkledger-20) cleaning consumes 15-20% of ink capacity annually.
3. **Laser Reliability**: No printhead clogs mean fewer service interruptions for laser users.

### When Inkjet Makes Sense
- Photo/color printing needs
- Very low volume (under 200 pages/month)
- Need all-in-one scanning features

For most home offices printing primarily documents, the [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01LYFVG1V?tag=inkledger-20) laser's $1,005 savings over 3 years makes it our recommended choice.
