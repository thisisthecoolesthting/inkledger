## Why HP Cartridges Stop Printing Prematurely

Before you assume your HP ink [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GLTX2PNR?tag=inkledger-20) is empty, check for these common flow blockers:

1. **Clogged Nozzles** - Dried ink accumulates in the print head. Symptoms: Streaky prints or missing colors.
2. **Air Bubbles** - Disrupted ink channels from rough handling. Symptoms: Intermittent printing.
3. **Firmware Glitches** - False 'empty' alerts. Symptoms: [Cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01BYKUI9C?tag=inkledger-20) stops despite visible ink.

## Step-by-Step Fixes

### For Clogged Nozzles
- Run 2-3 head cleaning cycles (found in your [printer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B077QVSKD5?tag=inkledger-20)'s maintenance menu)
- If streaks remain, dampen a lint-free cloth with distilled water and gently wipe the [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FKBFH92Q?tag=inkledger-20) nozzles
- Let dry completely before reinstalling

### For Air Bubbles
- Remove [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B081GRVJ5K?tag=inkledger-20) and gently tap it nozzle-side down on a soft surface
- Reinstall and run a cleaning cycle
- For stubborn cases: Wrap nozzles in damp paper towel for 10 minutes to rehydrate ink channels

### When All Else Fails
- Try these [third-party refill kits](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0G1R37VJC?tag=inkledger-20/?tag=inkledger-20) for HP 61/62/63 cartridges
- Consider switching to [high-yield HP 962XL](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FVY8TTVN?tag=inkledger-20/?tag=inkledger-20) for better reliability

**Pro Tip:** Store spare cartridges in a sealed bag with a damp paper towel to prevent drying.

*Note: These methods work for most HP thermal inkjet models. For laser printers or [EcoTank](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B4VP5CQ4?tag=inkledger-20) systems, see our [printer maintenance guide](#).*
