When shopping for an affordable laser [printer](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B077QVSKD5?tag=inkledger-20), the [Brother HL-L2350DW](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00MFG54B6?tag=inkledger-20) and HP [LaserJet](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B09N4Z8DNL?tag=inkledger-20) M110w often top the under-$200 lists. But which one actually saves you money long-term? We dug into the real costs beyond the sticker price.

## Upfront Price Comparison
- **[Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B06XY6RSNB?tag=inkledger-20) HL-L2350DW**: Typically $150-$180 (frequent sales)
- **HP [LaserJet](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FNMZCLCG?tag=inkledger-20) M110w**: Usually $20-$50 more than [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01N4K0ZFE?tag=inkledger-20)

Both printers go on sale regularly, but [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DS4M632H?tag=inkledger-20) maintains a consistent price advantage. Watch for HP's artificial price inflation - the M110w often spikes to $249 before 'discounting' to $199.

## Toner Costs: The Hidden Battle
Here's where [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GFMH3D3X?tag=inkledger-20) pulls ahead:

| Metric               | [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B01LYFVG1V?tag=inkledger-20) TN-660 | HP 83A (CF283A) |
|----------------------|----------------|-----------------|
| OEM Toner Price      | $50-$60        | $60-$75         |
| Page Yield           | 1,200          | 1,000           |
| Cost Per Page        | 4.1¢           | 6.0¢+           |

Third-party options widen the gap further. [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B075X6C5ZW?tag=inkledger-20)-[compatible](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQ4VHBMH?tag=inkledger-20) toners start at $15 (1.2¢/page) while HP's DRM makes cheap alternatives unreliable.

## Reliability & Features
- **[Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07YGL9XTH?tag=inkledger-20)**: No DRM on toner, duplex printing standard
- **HP**: Optional duplex add-on costs $50+, toner DRM locks

Both printers handle 25+ pages per minute, but [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FLXJHGR7?tag=inkledger-20)'s 250-sheet paper tray beats HP's 150-sheet capacity. Neither supports automatic document feeders for scanning.

## Verdict
For home offices printing 100-300 pages/month, the [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FT39TGXN?tag=inkledger-20) HL-L2350DW delivers:
- 30% lower cost per page
- More reliable third-party toner options
- Standard duplex printing

HP's M110w only makes sense if you find it under $150 and commit to HP Instant Ink (which has its own limitations). Otherwise, [Brother](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DJ79LQP5?tag=inkledger-20)'s ecosystem saves $100+ annually on supplies.
