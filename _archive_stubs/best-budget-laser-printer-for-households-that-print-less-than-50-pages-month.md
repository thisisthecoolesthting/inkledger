## Why Laser Beats Inkjet for Light Printing

Laser printers dominate for low-volume households because:

- **No dried ink**: [Toner](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B075X6C5ZW?tag=inkledger-20) doesn't clog if unused for weeks
- **Lower cost per page**: Even OEM [toner](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07YGL9XTH?tag=inkledger-20) averages 3¢/page vs. 15¢+ for inkjet
- **No waste**: Inkjet 'cleaning cycles' can waste 30% of your [cartridge](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GLTX2PNR?tag=inkledger-20)
