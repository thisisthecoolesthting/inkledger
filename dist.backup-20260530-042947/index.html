About InkLedger

Why InkLedger exists

One household, 12 years of ink receipts, and the slow realization that the printer was free and the cartridges cost more than a car payment. InkLedger is the site we wish had existed then. We do the pricing work so you can buy less ink for less money, or ditch the inkjet entirely for the tasks that do not actually need one.
