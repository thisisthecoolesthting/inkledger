Marcus Nolan leads InkLedger's cartridge testing and retail price tracking. Before InkLedger, he worked on office-products pricing and yield analysis for a national distributor — the job that made him angry enough to build a public watchdog.

Every pick on this site goes through retail purchase, a 200-page mixed print workload, and weekly price re-checks. Marcus does not accept OEM sponsorships or paid placement.

When he is not printing test grids, he is answering reader mail about Instant Ink math and whether your household should exit inkjet entirely for a mono laser.
