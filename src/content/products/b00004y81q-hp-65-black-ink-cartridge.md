# HP 65 Black Ink — Cost Per Page Analysis

The HP 65 Black yields ~120 pages at standard coverage. At $17.99 that's **$0.15/page** — one of the most expensive cost-per-page figures in the HP lineup.

## Standard vs. XL comparison

| Cartridge | Yield | Price | Cost/page |
|---|---|---|---|
| HP 65 Standard | ~120 pages | $17.99 | $0.15 |
| HP 65XL Black | ~300 pages | $26.99 | $0.09 |
| Compatible (generic) | ~200 pages | $8.99 | $0.045 |

**The XL is almost always worth it** if you print more than once a week. The standard only makes sense if you rarely print and want a small cartridge for emergency use.

## Compatible alternatives

Third-party HP 65-compatible cartridges are widely available for $6–$10. Print quality is indistinguishable for text documents; photos may show slight color variance.
