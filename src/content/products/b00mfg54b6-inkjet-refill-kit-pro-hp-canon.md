# Inkjet Refill Kit Pro — Does DIY Ink Actually Work?

Refilling your own cartridges with the Inkjet Refill Kit Pro costs roughly **$0.004/page** vs. $0.10–$0.15 for OEM cartridges — a 25–37× savings.

## What's in the kit

- 4 × 30ml ink bottles (black, cyan, magenta, yellow)
- Refill needles and plugs
- Instructions for HP, Canon, Epson, Lexmark formats

## Real-world test results

Over 500 test pages across HP DeskJet and Canon PIXMA cartridges:
- **Text documents:** Output quality matched OEM on all tests
- **Photos:** Slight magenta shift on Canon; acceptable on HP
- **Clogging:** 2 out of 8 refilled cartridges clogged after 2nd refill

## Who should use it

Best for text-heavy printing households. If you print photos regularly, stick to OEM or compatible cartridges — the color fidelity difference is visible.

## Clogging prevention tip

Run a print-head cleaning cycle immediately after refilling and again after 48 hours of non-use.
