## Why Laser Beats Inkjet for Light Printing

Laser printers dominate for low-volume households because:

- **No dried ink**: Toner doesn't clog if unused for weeks
- **Lower cost per page**: Even OEM toner averages 3¢/page vs. 15¢+ for inkjet
- **No waste**: Inkjet 'cleaning cycles' can waste 30% of your cartridge
