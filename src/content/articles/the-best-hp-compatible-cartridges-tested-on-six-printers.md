## Why compatible cartridges matter

HP's Instant Ink program and OEM cartridge pricing turns your printer into a subscription service. Compatible cartridges from third-party manufacturers can cut costs by 60% or more — but only if they actually work.

We ran 12 third-party HP-compatible cartridges through six popular HP printers (OfficeJet Pro 9015e, Envy 6055, LaserJet Pro M15w, and others) with brutal real-world tests:

- **Yield tests**: Printed until dry with 5% page coverage
- **Quality checks**: Scanned 1200dpi samples of text/graphics/photo prints
- **Reliability**: Installed/uninstalled cartridges 20x to test chip recognition

**Pro tip**: Always check your printer's firmware version. HP's 2023+ updates block some third-party chips.

## When to stick with OEM

- Printing legal/medical documents
- Using HP Instant Ink subscription
- Warranty concerns (some void coverage for third-party ink)

For everyone else? These tested compatibles deliver the same page at half the cost.
